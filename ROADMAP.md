## Long-Term Vision

52! is not intended to become only a card shuffling tool.

Its long-term vision is to become a small open-source framework for building meaningful micro-experiences around:

- probability
- attention
- impermanence
- uniqueness
- randomness
- contemplative design
- philosophical interaction
- educational visualization

The project should remain simple, lightweight, privacy-conscious, and easy to remix.
